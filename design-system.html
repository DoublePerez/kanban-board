DESIGN SYSTEM

Dither Kanban

Token-based design system for a glassmorphic kanban board. Every surface, color, and spacing value is drawn from a single source of truth. Dark theme only.

White borders at low opacity define edges without hard lines. Borders get stronger on interactive elements and focus states.
